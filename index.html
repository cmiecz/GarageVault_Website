Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GarageVault

What does GarageVault do?

GarageVault uses AI to organize your garage and track inventory. Take a photo of any tool, paint can, or equipment - the AI identifies it instantly and extracts brand, size, and color automatically. No typing required! You'll get alerts when you're near hardware stores reminding you what you need to buy.

How does AI identification work?

Simply take a photo and our AI instantly identifies the item, extracting details like brand, size, and color automatically. It works with tools, paint, fasteners, equipment, and more - no manual typing needed!

What's included in the free version?

The free version lets you track up to 10 items with full AI identification, photo uploads, and location tracking. It's perfect for testing all core features before upgrading.

How does family sharing work?

Premium members can share their inventory with their entire household in real-time. Everyone sees what's in stock, so no more "I didn't know we had that!" Check if there's paint before heading to the store. You control exactly what you share.

What stores support location reminders?

Premium members get alerts when near Home Depot, Lowe's, or their local hardware stores. For example: "You're near Lowe's - you need deck screws!" You can check your inventory before shopping and quick reorder right from the app.

How do QR codes work?

Premium members can generate QR labels for toolboxes, shelves, and cabinets. Just scan any container to instantly see what's inside. Assign storage locations to every item and find anything in seconds.

How much does Premium cost?

Premium is $4.99/month or $39.99/year (save 33%). Payment is charged to your Apple Account at confirmation and auto-renews unless canceled 24 hours before the period ends. You can manage your subscription in App Store Account Settings.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely! Your data is private household data only with secure cloud sync. We never sell your data - ever. You control what you share with family members. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I track warranties and maintenance?

Yes! Premium members can track warranties, purchase dates, set low-stock thresholds for essentials, schedule equipment maintenance, and log service history - all in one place.
